As a newcomer you should:

  1. Not commit to more than 3 points of tasks during your first week, or 5 points your second week. (It's really easy to over-commit at first, so keep your commitments manageable. You can always take on more work later in the sprint if you finish early.)
  2. Provide lots of updates to your assigned reviewer/mentor for each task, so they can provide timely help.
