I do more or less what Brandon does, except that I use GTimer instead of Hamster. It's quite simple, but rather idiosyncratic, and the only reason I'm using it is because the Xubuntu Hamster package was broken at the time I looked for a time tracking tool and I could not be bothered to compile it myself.

For small tasks, I just start a stopwatch on my phone and enter the time directly into Jira once I'm done. This may seem somewhat crude, but it's still a lot better than the Tempo time tracker on Jira.
