Note: the subteam owner is the first/leftmost person listed next to the subteam name on the Weekly Sprint board . When that person is away, the next leftmost person listed there will be the acting subteam owner.

As a subteam owner:

  1. I will make sure the subteam has groomed and assigned all of its stories before Monday's meeting. (I will use the asynchronous grooming tool to let the subteam help groom the stories, unless the pointing of the stories is very obvious/non-controversial or there is not enough time for asynchronous grooming session.).
  2. I will aim for the subteam's volume of work each month to match the monthly hour goals (not too low nor too high) - except for the small projects subteam where this doesn't apply.
