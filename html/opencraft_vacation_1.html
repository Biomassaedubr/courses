To request time off please:

  1. Make sure to book holidays from two weeks in advance (for a few days) to a month (for 1+ weeks).
  2. Check the team calendar first to see if anyone else has booked those dates off - we try to avoid having more than two people on vacation at the same time.
  3. Check if you need to make any trades on the rotations schedule and if so, get tentative agreement from someone to trade with you.
  4. For all subteams other than small projects: find someone from the small projects team who agrees to cover for your time commitments on each of your subteams.
  5. Make sure that the epic reviewer for any epic you own is going to be present and is fully informed so they can take over the epic owner duties while you are away.
  6. Email Xavier to ask him to confirm your time off.
  7. Before leaving for vacation, make sure to set a vacation responder indicating dates you will be unavailable and an alternative person and / or email to contact in case of urgency (notify the person(s) covering for you). Remember to turn the responder off once back to work! Please find instructions on how to do it here .
